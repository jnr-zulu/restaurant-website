Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

+

Yes, we accept reservations for all party sizes. For parties larger than 8, we recommend calling us directly at 0822166658. You can also make a reservation online through our reservations page.

Do you offer delivery?

+

Yes, we offer delivery within the White River area. Delivery is free for orders over R250. You can place an order for delivery through our Order Online page.

Do you cater for events?

+

Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your catering needs.

Are you open on public holidays?

+

Our hours may vary on public holidays. Please check our social media accounts or call us directly to confirm our holiday hours.
